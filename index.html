About

The song "family ties" was released August 27, 2021 as the fourth single from the album "the meldoic blue". The song recived two nominations at the 2022 Grammy Awards for Best Rap Song and Best Rap Performance. It won the Grammy for Best Rap Performance. The song went platinum 5x in the United States of America.

trademark usa

"Trademark USA" is the opening track of Baby Keem’s 2021 album The Melodic Blue. The song features an evolving beat, switching from an introspective tone to high-energy flows. Keem reflects on his rise to fame, personal struggles, and newfound success, delivering raw lyrics with his signature unpredictable delivery. The track sets the tone for the album, showcasing his versatility and unique artistic style.

range brothers

"Range Brothers" by Baby Keem, featuring Kendrick Lamar, is a track from Keem’s 2021 album The Melodic Blue. The song is known for its unconventional structure, shifting beats, and distinct vocal deliveries. It explores themes of success, family, and resilience, with verses exchanged in a theatrical manner. The song gained attention for its unique ad-libs and the now-iconic "top of the morning" repetition by Kendrick in the latter half.

16

"16" is the closing track on Baby Keem’s 2021 album The Melodic Blue. The song features a melancholic and dreamy production, with Keem delivering introspective lyrics about love, heartbreak, and emotional struggles. His melodic flow and vulnerable tone contrast with the more energetic tracks on the album, making "16" a reflective and atmospheric finale.
